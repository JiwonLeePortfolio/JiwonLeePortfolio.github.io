About Me

I am currently a junior majoring in Computer Science B.S. at Texas A&M University. I started my computer science journey during high school after taking an intro to computer science class. Signing up for the class, I didn't fully understand what Computer Science was exactly and how pivotal it was to our history. After writing my first program, I thought programming was just learning a new language in which you can speak to computers. However, I now know that being able to speak to computers is a very valuable skill that can do much to exponentiate your efficiency and creativity. Looking back, I am glad to be studying in a field that offers diversity in what you can work with.

In a working environment, I enjoy collaborating with others and listening to their point of view because it always allows the group to observe the problem in a different way.
